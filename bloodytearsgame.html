Game: Bloody Tears

A game can be defined as a form of play that is engaging to the person and involves various forms of interactive activities. Higher focus on detail, deeper storytelling, and more engaging tasks to complete are what make games more intriguing. 

The simplistic game I have devised is titled "Bloody Tears"—a real life phenomenon caused by higher blood pressure, inflammation, or psychiatric disorders; it also emphasizes on the despair and sorrow associated with tears, as these tears are not simply made of the usual watery lipids. In the game, the player collects bloody tears as they drag their mouse to move the box—then, the speed increases. If the player misses even one bloody tear, the game ends; loading a screen that remarks, "TOO SLOW! GAME OVER." Overall, the bloody tears are meant to represent the feelings of a gamer, as the game becomes more difficult—resulting in them entering a trance of despair.
